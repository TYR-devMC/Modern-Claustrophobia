"scavenge_world:gravity"
Moves the structure to the lowest ground

"heightMap": the Height Map that should be referenced (Valid Values: [WORLD_SURFACE_WG, WORLD_SURFACE, OCEAN_FLOOR_WG, OCEAN_FLOOR, MOTION_BLOCKING, MOTION_BLOCKING_NO_LEAVES])
"offset": The Offset that is applied on top of the current height (Optional, Default: 0)
